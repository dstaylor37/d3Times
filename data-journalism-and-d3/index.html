Correlations Discovered Between Obesity and Smoking

The recent release of the 2014 ACS 1-year estimates reveals strong correation between smoking and obesity. In reviewing certain demographic characterstics at the state level, the American Community Survey (ACS) dataset released recently by the US Census, reveals a strong relationship between obesity and smoking. The strong positive relationship strongly suggests as rates of smoking increase, so do rates of obesity at the state level. Each marker represents a state, so click to see the relationships at the state level. The correlation between obesity and smoking is 0.74. As a reminder, this relationship does not suggest causality.
